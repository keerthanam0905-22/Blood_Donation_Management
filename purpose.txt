Blood Donation Management System (Search + Sort)
1. Problem Statement
    -Hospitals and blood banks often face difficulty in:
    -Finding matching blood donors quickly
    -Managing donor records
    -Tracking blood stock availability
    -Handling emergency blood requests

System Objectives
The system must:
    1.Register blood donors
    2.Store blood group information
    3.Handle emergency blood requests
    4.Match compatible donors
    5.Maintain blood inventory
    6.Generate reports
    7.Store data permanently (File/SQLite)

Functional Requirements
A. Donor Management
    •Add donor (Name, Age, Blood Group, Contact, Last Donation Date)
    •Update donor details
    •Delete donor
    •Search donor by:
        -Blood group
        -Location (optional advanced feature)
        -unrecent donation(before 5 months)

B. Blood Inventory Management
    •Add blood units
    •Remove blood units
    •Display available stock by blood group
    •Alert if stock is low

C. Emergency Request Handling
    •Add patient request
    •Match compatible donors
    •Maintain waiting list if blood unavailable(Emergency color )

D. Reports
    •Total donors
    •Blood stock summary
    •Most requested blood group
    •Donation history
